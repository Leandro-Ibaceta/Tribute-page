Hideo Kojima

The man behind the metal gear solid series and the award wining Death Stranding

Face of Hideo Kojima

Here are some interesting things he made:

  • In 1986 he write and design Metal Gear for the MSX2, which basiclly lay the foundation of the stealth genre.
  • In 1998 Metal Gear Solid was launch, making him one of the most acclaim videogame designers of the time.
  • In 2005 Kojima Productions was found, a studio controlled by Konami.
  • In 2015 Hideo Kojima left Konami along with his studio and begin working on a new title.
  • In 2016 was awarded the honorary icon of the video game industry award.
  • In 2019 Kojima won the best direction award in The Game Award gala with his new indie title Death Stranding.
